Urinal

Urinal is commenting on gender norms in America. It was set up in a girls bathroom, next to a tampon machine. Free vs not free?? The tampon machine is higher on the wall, depicting the female gendered object as higher than the male gendered object, as a critisism of the typical heirarchy of genders.
